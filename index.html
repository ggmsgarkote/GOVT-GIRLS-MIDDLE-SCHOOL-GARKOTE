Welcome to Our School

Govt Girls Middle School Garkote is dedicated to providing quality education and nurturing a love for learning in every student. We focus on academic excellence, moral values, and holistic development.

Admission

Admission is open for all eligible girls from Grades 1 to 8. Interested parents can collect admission forms from the school office or contact us for more information.

Result

Annual and term results are displayed on the school notice board and shared with parents. Online result access will be available soon.

Attendance

Regular attendance is essential for academic success. Attendance reports are maintained by class teachers and shared monthly with parents.
